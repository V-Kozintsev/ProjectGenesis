# Game Design Document

## Genre

Fantasy RPG prototype with long-term online RPG direction.

## Camera And Control

Initial control target:

- third-person character movement;
- orbit camera;
- mouse and keyboard first;
- controller support later;
- click-to-target or soft targeting can be evaluated after basic combat works.

## Main Gameplay Loop

1. Player receives a goal from an NPC or personal progression need.
2. Player leaves the safe zone.
3. Player fights enemies or gathers objects.
4. Player gains experience and loot.
5. Player returns to safety.
6. Player upgrades equipment, skills, or quest state.
7. New area or harder enemy becomes reasonable.

## Player Lifecycle

### Start

The player creates a character with:

- race;
- class;
- name;
- basic appearance later.

### Early Game

The player learns:

- movement;
- camera;
- interaction;
- basic attack;
- health;
- enemy threat;
- inventory;
- quest turn-in.

### Mid Prototype

The player unlocks:

- multiple skills;
- equipment slots;
- class identity;
- elite enemies;
- a cave or dungeon-like area;
- save/load.

## World Structure

Initial playable world:

- starter village;
- training field;
- forest road;
- wolf/boar area;
- cave entrance;
- small boss encounter.

## Success Criteria For Version 0.1

Version 0.1 is successful when the player can:

- create or load a character;
- move in a starter scene;
- interact with at least one NPC;
- accept and complete one quest;
- fight at least one enemy type;
- gain experience;
- receive loot;
- equip one item;
- save and reload core progress.
